Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my budget?

Tap the Settings icon on the dashboard to return to the setup wizard. Saving a new configuration will update your budget.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What happens if I uninstall the app?

All data will be permanently deleted. There is currently no backup or export feature.

Will cloud sync be available?

Cloud sync is planned for a future release.
