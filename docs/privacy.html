Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Expense Macros ("the App") is a budgeting tool that runs entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Storage

All budget configurations and transaction data are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. No data is sent to any server.

No Analytics

The App does not use any analytics, tracking, or crash reporting services.

No Accounts

The App does not require or support user accounts, login, or authentication of any kind.

Data Deletion

Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all locally stored data. There is no backup or recovery mechanism.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@expensemacros.app.
